Austin GC Questions

What areas in Austin do you serve?

We serve the entire Austin metro including Austin, Round Rock, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, Leander, Kyle, Buda, and Lakeway. If your project is in the Austin metro, we can get there.

What is the typical lead time for an Austin court surfacing project?

For Austin-area projects, we typically have a 2-3 week lead time from contract to mobilization, depending on the season. Once on site, most amenity center courts take 5-10 business days to complete. We provide an exact timeline in our bid so you can schedule your other trades around us.

Do you handle tennis court resurfacing in Austin?

Yes. Tennis court resurfacing is one of our core services in Austin. We resurface existing courts with ATS Sports Acrytech acrylic systems -- patching cracks, leveling low spots, and applying a full new color coat with fresh game lines. The result is a surface that plays like new and holds up to the Austin heat.

Can you install pickleball courts at Austin amenity centers?

Absolutely. Pickleball court surfacing is a growing part of our Austin work. We install dedicated pickleball courts and convert existing tennis courts to dual-use tennis/pickleball layouts with proper line striping for both sports.

What warranty do you provide on Austin court projects?

Every Austin project gets two layers of coverage: the ATS Sports Acrytech manufacturer warranty on materials, plus our 2-year workmanship warranty on the install. Both sets of documentation go straight into your closeout package.

How fast can you turn around a bid for an Austin project?

We turn around detailed, line-item bids within 48 hours for Austin-area projects. Send us court dimensions, surface type, color preferences, and your project timeline. If you have site plans, even better.
