Frequently Asked Questions
Questions GCs Ask Us

What surface system do you install?

We install ATS Sports Acrytech — a professional-grade acrylic sport court surface system. It's engineered for high-traffic environments like multi-family amenity centers and holds up to the Central Texas heat and UV exposure.

Do you work as a subcontractor under a GC?

Yes. That's our primary model. We carry our own insurance, provide W-9s, and work within your project management structure. We're used to GC workflows — submittals, schedules, site meetings, all of it.

What do you need from us before you can bid?

Court dimensions, surface type (tennis, pickleball, multi-sport), color preferences, and your project timeline. If you have site plans, even better — send them over and we'll turn around a bid in 48 hours.

How long does a typical court surfacing job take?

Most amenity center courts take 5-10 business days depending on court count and weather. We'll give you an exact timeline in our bid so you can plan your schedule around it.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the Central Texas region — Austin, San Antonio, Waco, and surrounding areas. If your project is in Central Texas, we can get there.

Are your surfaces warrantied?

Yes. You get two layers of coverage: ATS Sports Acrytech's manufacturer warranty on materials, plus our 2-year workmanship warranty on the install. Both sets of documentation go straight into your project closeout package.
