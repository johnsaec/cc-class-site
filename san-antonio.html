Questions San Antonio GCs Ask Us

Who does tennis court resurfacing in San Antonio?

Pro Court Surfaces handles tennis court resurfacing across San Antonio and the surrounding metro — New Braunfels, Boerne, Schertz, and the full I-35 corridor. We install ATS Sports Acrytech acrylic surfaces and work as a sub under your GC.

How much does court resurfacing cost in San Antonio?

Pricing depends on court count, surface condition, and court type. Rather than a vague range, we provide detailed line-item bids within 48 hours — surface prep, materials, labor, and striping broken out so you have real numbers for your estimate.

Do you install pickleball courts in San Antonio?

Yes. We install pickleball court surfaces for multi-family amenity centers and commercial projects across the SA metro. ATS Sports Acrytech surfacing, color coating, and line striping — all to PPA spec.

What warranty do you offer on San Antonio court projects?

Two layers: ATS Sports Acrytech's manufacturer warranty on materials, plus our 2-year workmanship warranty on the install. Both sets of documentation go straight into your closeout package.

What areas around San Antonio do you cover?

Greater SA metro including New Braunfels, San Marcos, Boerne, Helotes, Schertz, Cibolo, and Seguin. We run the I-35 corridor from SA to Austin, so if your project is anywhere in between, we've got it covered.

Can you handle both my San Antonio and Austin projects?

Absolutely. We serve the full Central Texas region. One sub, one bid process, consistent quality across both markets. No need to vet separate surfacing crews for each city.

How does the San Antonio heat affect court surfaces?

San Antonio's UV exposure and summer temps are brutal on cheap surfaces. ATS Acrytech is engineered for thermal expansion and UV stability — it's the same system used on commercial courts across the Sun Belt. No bubbling, no premature failure.
